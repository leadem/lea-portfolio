Promotions

As part of the rebranding effort, I had to do an integral redesign of their catalogue and other brochures. The main challenge was keeping the catalogue pocket size whilst still displaying the full product range in the best light.

Black Friday

Black Friday is a very important sale for the company. I kept a dark tone that was very important to the company that I mixed with a more punchy colour to still attract the eyes.

Combine & Save

The company sometimes run two promotions simultaneously. The challenge was to make them work together while being contrasting enough to convey a clear message.

Everything We Do

The challenge here was to work with a wordy promotional name on a restricted layout. I used the blank space as a graphic component to give structure and hierarchy to the message.

Birthday Sale

For their birthday, I came up with a festive yet abstract theme. The difficulty was to create a bokeh effect on decals playing with layers and transparency.

Summer Sale

For Summer Sale, we needed a different and punchy promotion. I tried something new with this long decal block. It got mixed reviews as it was too obstructing for small stores.
