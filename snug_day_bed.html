Snug Day Bed

Day beds have been the best selling product of the company. The brief was to come up with a completely new look to add to the collection. The original day beds have their armrest at 45° and flat. With the new one, I added a 90° angle armrest position for a more compact and modern style. I also updated the back seat with two plush doted cushions. The frame had an upgrade too with new rounded corners while still keeping production cost down.
